    Project Overview

    Avadhani Warehousing, Is Another First Of Its Kind Warehousing And Storage Facility Designed With Optimum Space Utilisation And All Designed Around Harvesting Wind And Solar Energy.


    Located In Koorgalli Industrial Area, Mysore, The Location Has Excellent Access To All Major Cities In Karnataka, Including Bangalore And Well Connected Via All Major National Highways.


    Avadhani Warehousing Offers Fragmented 15000 Sft Single Unit To 60000 Sft Unified Total Storage Facility, Which Is Available For Immediate Rental.


    The Warehousing Uses Maximum Of Sun Harvesting, With Ample Parking Space Around The Site With Easy Entry And Exit For Large Vehicles. The Facility Comes With Dedicated Cafeteria And Toilet Facility From Outside.

    Concept

    Sprawling Over 2 Acres Of Land With Substantial Slope, The Site Is Housed With A Large Storage Facility Which Internally Is Divided Into 04 Bays, The Roof Of The Facility Along With The Exhaust Turbines Has Accomadated 20% Of Light Bays For Use Of Natural Light, And Steps Down To Create A Unique Architectural Statement.

    The Storage Facility Comes With Maximum Height For Pallete Storage, High Dock Loading, And Has Sufficient Parking Space For Cars And Trucks, It Is Designed For Independent Office Wing, Pantry And Toilets. If Desired By End Users.
